About ISMAA Bengaluru

Indian School of Mines (IIT ISM), Dhanbad

"अतिष्ठ जागत प्राप्त वरान्निबोधत" - Rise, Awake and Achieve Excellence

Established in 1926, the Indian School of Mines is one of India's premier technical institutions.

Originally founded as the Indian School of Mines to cater to the mining industry, it has evolved into IIT ISM Dhanbad, offering world-class education in engineering, technology, and management. The institution has produced thousands of successful alumni who have made significant contributions across various industries globally.

Our Mission

To foster lifelong connections among Indian School of Mines alumni in Bengaluru, creating a vibrant community that supports professional growth, networking, and meaningful relationships.

Our Vision

To be the premier alumni network in Bengaluru, facilitating knowledge exchange, mentorship opportunities, and collaborative initiatives that benefit both our members and the broader community.

Our Objectives

The ISMAA Bengaluru Chapter is established with the following core objectives to serve our alumni community and contribute to society:

Alumni Networking

To provide a forum for Bangalore based Alumni to connect with each other and foster strong relationships within our community.

Institute Connection

To foster a strong bond with the Institute and offer support to ISM/IIT chapters across different locations.

Professional Development

To provide professional networking among alumni by organizing lectures, debates, seminars and conducting sessions on diverse topics of expertise.

Entrepreneurship Support

To guide and mentor Bangalore based Alumni entrepreneurs in their pursuit of creating valuable products and services pertaining to their area of expertise.

Community Welfare

To promote friendship and welfare amongst the members and to provide funding through contributions by members for volunteering activities.

Student Support

To help needy students of all communities at the Institute to pursue their studies through various support mechanisms.

Philanthropic Activities

To engage and assist in philanthropic activities as may be deemed appropriate by the Governing Body of the society, contributing to the broader community welfare.

Our Commitment

Through these objectives, ISMAA Bengaluru Chapter strives to create a lasting impact on our alumni community, the institute, and society at large. We believe in the power of collective effort and shared values to achieve meaningful progress.
